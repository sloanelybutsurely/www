# Sloane Perrault

I'm a software engineer and technical leader. I currently serve as CTO and
co-founder of [screen.garden], a real-time collaboration and sync service for
[Obsidian]. I care about building reliable systems, leading engineers, and
shipping things that help people in their daily lives.

Outside of work I enjoy singing in a [community chorus][cgmc], wrenching on my
Miata, listening to music, and [reading books][storygraph].

You can find me on [GitHub], [LinkedIn], [Bluesky], [Instagram], or [reach out
via email][email].
